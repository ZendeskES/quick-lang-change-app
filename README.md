# Quick Lang Change app

App that allows you to easily switch languages of your account without having to visit your profile.

### Info of the app

App is located on the top bar. It has the following icon: ![image_1](https://cloud.githubusercontent.com/assets/8858963/13327530/2ecd45dc-dbe2-11e5-840f-32ee7ce409e4.png) 

The app will show a list of favorite languages and will also show a full list of available languages that you can switch to.
